About Us

Based in Paris, France. Our mission is to promote the skills, culture and traditions of the traveling circus, and to inspire youth to engage in life-changing adventures in the circus arts.
Since 1889, Wild Circus has been bringing the transforming power of the circus arts to people of all ages.
We have many animals like camels, elephants, tigers, lions, horses and many more.
